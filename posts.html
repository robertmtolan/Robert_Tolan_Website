Whale Watching

Spending some time in credit derivatives got me interested in when things go wrong and one of the more intriguing cases is 2012's London Whale. The main characters are Bruno Iksil and JP Morgan, the loss led to court cases. At one stage, Iksil ran a blog called the Marionette which told his side of the story which has since been largely ignored by the media. Some month's prior to the blog going offline, I downloaded its contents. I am making them available here for posterity.
