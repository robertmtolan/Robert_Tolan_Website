Housing Policy

  • During a gap year in 2022/3, I founded the Better Planning Alliance to research the potential of supply-side housing policies in Ireland. I modelled a number of proposed policies, including Street Plans and Accessory Dwelling Units, that could help ease Ireland's housing shortage. This was inspired by the Housing Theory of Everything which states the developed world's ailments are easily treated by solving housing shortages, an idea due to John Myers, Sam Bowman and Ben Southwood. Reforming planning in Ireland has the potential to turn development from a zero-sum to a positive-sum game.   • The project led to my being quoted in The Economist on Irish housing policy and some coverage in the Irish Times as well as The Currency.
  • Researching such policies from a farm in the West of Ireland involved extensive use of Google Earth, Google Maps, and spreadsheets in addition to giving me exposure to civic hacking, fundraising, and alliance building.
