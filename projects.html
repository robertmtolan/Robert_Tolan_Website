Housing Policy

  • During a gap year in 2022/3, I founded the Better Planning Alliance to research the potential of supply-side housing policies in Ireland. I modelled a number of proposed policies, including Street Plans and Accessory Dwelling Units, that could help ease Ireland's housing shortage. This was inspired by the Housing Theory of Everything which states the developed world's ailments are easily treated by solving housing shortages, an idea due to John Myers, Sam Bowman and Ben Southwood. Reforming planning in Ireland has the potential to turn development from a zero-sum to a positive-sum game.
